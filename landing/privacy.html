2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data: email address, password (hashed), subscription plan and status

  • Payment data: billing information processed by Stripe (we do not store card numbers)
  • Usage data: reel generation history, credit usage, session activity
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, access timestamps
3. How We Use Your Data
  • Providing and operating the DELULUREEL service
  • Processing payments and managing your subscription
  • Generating video reels from your uploaded content via AI video models
  • Sending transactional emails (account confirmation, payment receipts, subscription reminders)
  • Improving the service and diagnosing technical issues
  • Complying with legal obligations
4. Third-Party Services

DELULUREEL relies on the following third-party processors to deliver its service:

  • Supabase (auth, database, file storage) — Privacy Policy
  • Stripe (payment processing) — Privacy Policy
  • fal.ai (AI video generation — Kling AI) — Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (AI scene direction — Claude) — Privacy Policy
  • Fly.io (hosting and compute infrastructure) — Privacy Policy
  • Resend (transactional email delivery) — Privacy Policy

Your uploaded files (audio and photos) are transmitted to fal.ai solely for the purpose of video generation, then deleted. We do not use your content to train AI models.

5. Data Retention
  • Uploaded audio/photos: deleted within 24 hours of reel generation
  • Generated reels: retained for the duration of your active subscription; deleted within 30 days of account closure
